Winner!

Tic Tac Toe

How to Play:

1.Take Turns: Click on an empty square to place your "X" or "O".

2.Win the Game: Get three of your marks in a row.

3.Enjoy and Repeat: Have fun playing again and again!

How to Win:

  • Three in a Row: To win the game, you need to get three of your marks (either "X" or "O") in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally on the grid.

  • Horizontal Win: If you place your marks in three consecutive cells on the same row, you win horizontally.

  • Vertical Win: If you place your marks in three consecutive cells on the same column, you win vertically.

  • Diagonal Win: If you place your marks in three consecutive cells diagonally from one corner to another, you win diagonally.

  • When you achieve any of these patterns, the game will declare you as the winner!
